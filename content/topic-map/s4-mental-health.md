## Method Assignment Notes

- **Application stems dominant** (PLAN.md Evidence note 5), same as cc2 — this is a clinical-decision course. Definition-stem items are reserved for genuinely new classification content (Week 1 theories, mood/anxiety/psychotic disorder taxonomies).
- **Cloze** reserved for named values and relationships: drug onset windows (SSRIs 4–6 weeks, etc.), lithium therapeutic range, black-box warning triggers, DSM-5-TR criteria counts, CIWA/CAGE-type screening thresholds.
- **Free-recall** rationed per `FREE_RECALL_CAP = 3`/session — candidates flagged above (therapeutic relationships, trauma/crisis, mood/suicide, personality disorders, substance-use, eating disorders) are the highest-yield/highest-stability topics; actual authoring must ration to ≤1–2 per week's batch same as cc2.
- **Numeric** format is not used in this map — no dosage-calculation content is called out in the syllabus for this course; standing dosage-calc drill track (from `s1-dosage`) remains the numeric-format home.
- **No NGN cases.** Suicide-risk assessment, first-episode psychosis, and substance-withdrawal unfolding cases are natural Phase 3 candidates — note in authoring comments, do not build now.
